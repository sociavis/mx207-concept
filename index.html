AMA Featured Event

The Maine
Event 2026

When
August 28–29–30, 2026
Where
MX207 — Lyman, Maine
Format
3 Motos over 2 Days

The biggest stage in the Northeast to prove yourself and see how you match up against the best of the best. Three motos throughout the weekend — Saturday features all first motos and second motos until 6:45pm, Sunday continues with remaining motos and third motos.

Schedule

Thursday

Gates open at 9:00 AM. Track walk at 5:00 PM with Ty Lepicier from Moto Academy. Kids Zone at Kart Track 5:30–7:30 PM — music, bubbles, games, crafts.

Friday

Optional practice 9:00 AM – 3:30 PM ($45). Kids Zone 5:30–7:30 PM. Patra Live Show at 7:15 PM. Live music by Zac Herrin 8:00–9:00 PM.

Saturday

Riders meeting at 8:15 AM. Practice at 8:30 AM. Racing until 6:45 PM. Patra Live Show at 7:15 PM. Live music by Zac Herrin 8:00–9:00 PM.

Sunday

Riders meeting at 8:15 AM. Sight laps at 8:30 AM. Racing followed by awards.

Registration

Participants must have an AMA Membership and RaceReady profile to sign up. Online registration is available through iRaceReady. The first ten riders per class who sign up receive priority gate picks in registration order; remaining spots are randomly assigned (excluding 250 & 450 A classes).

Refund policy: Riders registering but not participating are eligible for 50% reimbursement until Saturday 6:00 AM.

Qualifier Format

Classes exceeding 47 riders run qualifiers, with heats determining the top 19 riders per class. Following all first motos, LCQs determine the top 5 additional qualifiers.

Pit Vehicles

Golf carts and pit bikes permitted with a $15 permit (riders must be 16+). ATVs, full-sized bikes, and race bikes are not allowed as pit vehicles. Operators must use a light when it gets dark. Pit vehicle permits are associated with a racer — misuse will result in penalty for the rider.

Golf carts available to rent through CCE's Rental Fleet — contact Beth at 508-322-3025.

Parking

Premium parking spots along the start straightaway are available through a lottery system. Standard parking spots are marked and riders can coordinate parking together.

Additional Info

  • Temporary AMA memberships available for $20
  • Riders may use a second bike if their primary breaks, provided it meets class criteria
